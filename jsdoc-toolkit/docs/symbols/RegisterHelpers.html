<private> _initialSetUp()
Parses the helpers folder, and requires each module in the helpers folder. The required module has two keys, ``helper`` - the name of helper needed to be registered and ``method`` the handler for that helper. With this info the helpers are registered in the handlebars module.
Throws:
{Error}
on fail of registering the helper, reasons could be an error in the require helper and also if the handlebars register method fails.
